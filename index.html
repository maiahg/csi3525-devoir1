About Me

Hi! I'm Mai Anh, currently exploring the world of UI/UX Design.

I'm a student at the University of Ottawa, majoring in Computer Science. I have a strong passion for design and development, and I believe that the best digital experiences are those that are both functional and beautiful. My background in UI/UX design, combined with my skills in Web Development, allows me to create user-friendly and visually appealing interfaces.

This portfolio is a space where I document my learning journey, showcase class projects, and reflect on how design thinking can solve real problems in elegant ways.

How I Work

I'm currently taking Analysis and Design of User Interfaces. Through this course, I'm learning the elements and principles of design, from visual hierarchy and typography to accessibility and user-centered design.

I'm also exploring concepts like:

  • Heuristic Evaluation
  • User-Centered Design
  • Wireframing and Prototyping
  • Principles of Visual Design

I often refer to trusted sources like Nielsen Norman Group (NN/g) to deepen my understanding and stay updated on best practices.

As I progress, I'll continue to refine my process and share how I go from sketches to polished prototypes , always keeping the user at the heart of each decision.
